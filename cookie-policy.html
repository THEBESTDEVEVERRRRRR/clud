Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

CloudCompute Pro uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This data helps us improve how our website works.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how our website is being used and to improve our services.

4. Specific Cookies We Use

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that set cookies on our website:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Payment Processors: Enable secure payment processing
  • Support Services: Provide customer support functionality

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings
  • Cookie Banner: Use our cookie consent banner to manage your preferences
  • Opt-out Links: Use third-party opt-out tools where available

7. Browser-Specific Cookie Management

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • You may need to re-enter information more frequently
  • Some personalization features may not work
  • Certain security features may be affected
  • We may not be able to remember your preferences

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
